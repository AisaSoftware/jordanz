Jordan Zimmerman has been playing and performing the drums and percussion in various contexts since he was a boy growing up in Los Angeles, California. As a child he studied with well known drum "guru" Freddie Gruber and played in the California State University at Northridge Youth Symphony. He attended the Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts. Before moving to Panama in 2013, he performed widely in California. While living in Panama, he notably performed at Danilo's Jazz Club and with Andrea Bocelli and the National Symphony of Panama. Jordan now lives with his wife near Lisbon, Portugal.
